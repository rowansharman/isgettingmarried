Gustavus is pretty small and has relatively few ammenities. It can be hard to get around without a car, it's uncertain whether/how many restaurants will be open, and there are a limited number of ways to even get there. With that in mind, we have come up with a kind of template itinerary for most folks, have a local caterer hired to feed people for the weekend, and have reserved the Gustavus Community Center (GCC).

IMPORTANT NOTE: GCC has requested no outdoor shoes in the building, so please bring a pair of comfortable indoor shoes (unless you're comfortable spending a lot of time on concrete in socks).

Here's how we envision the weekend!

Friday, June 16
Arrival Day

Attire
Whatever you're wearing

Most guests arrive in Gustavus on Alaska Airlines Flight 157, around 5 PM. They'll get to their lodging and from there go to the GCC (transportation TBD) for dinner. Pick up a sack breakfast for tomorrow.

Saturday, June 17th
Tour (or hangout) day

Attire
Layers and binoculars

This is your chance to see Glacier Bay! We recommend taking the Glacier Bay Lodge's tour boat (see Things to Do). It's an all day sort of deal. They give you lunch. We'll figure out transportation to/from Bartlett Cove. No rush to book. Prepare for glaciers and puffins.

Wedding prep and sandwiches at GCC for those who aren't on the boat. Dinner at GCC for everyone.

Sunday, June 18th
Wedding!

Attire
Whatever makes you feel confident and ready to dance! Bold colors appreciated.

This whole day happens at GCC. Brunch or breakfast (TBD) and dinner provided, with a ceremony somewhere in between. Big potluck (no need for out of town guests to bring anything), dancing (contra dance then club), music (live folk then party playlist), kegs, etc. We have two goals: Get married and have a big party!

Monday, June 19th
You're on your own

Attire
Comfy

You should have the lay of the land by now and be able to figure things out. Most people will probably leave on AK Air 77 around 5 pm.

We're sure a lot of you will want to extend the trip on one end or the other, so check out Things to Do for some tips.
